# 💸 Digital Twin as a Service (DTaaS)  
## Turning Engineering Expertise into a Scalable Asset

**AI Combiner** is a platform for creating **Digital Twins** — specialized AI agents that replicate real engineering expertise and operate as scalable, 24/7 digital professionals.

This document defines how expertise becomes:

- a **product**  
- a **revenue stream**  
- a **permanent organizational asset**  

---

# 🧠 What is a Digital Twin?

**Digital Twin** = an AI agent trained on:

- Real-world engineering cases  
- Validated solutions  
- Internal workflows and decision logic  

---

## ⚙️ Delivery Formats

- API / MCP agent  
- SaaS (limited, controlled exposure)  
- On-prem deployment (enterprise)  

---

# 📊 Unit Economics

## Core Formula
LTV = ARPU × Lifetime
CAC = Customer Acquisition Cost
LTV/CAC = Business Efficiency
---

## 📈 Benchmarks

| Segment | ARPU | Lifetime | LTV | CAC | LTV/CAC |
|--------|------|----------|-----|-----|---------|
| Expert | €20/mo | 12 mo | €240 | €20–50 | ~4–10x |
| Team | €500/mo | 18 mo | €9,000 | €500–1500 | ~6x |
| Enterprise | €50k/yr | 3 yrs | €150k | €5k–20k | ~7–20x |
