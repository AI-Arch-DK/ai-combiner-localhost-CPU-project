## Becoming a Maintainer

1. Contribute at least 3 accepted PRs
2. Demonstrate understanding of the routing architecture
3. Be nominated by the core maintainer
4. Accept responsibilities listed below

## Maintainer Responsibilities

- Review PRs within 7 days
- Triage new issues within 3 days
- Tag releases following `MAJOR.MINOR.PATCH` (see `docs/RELEASE.md`)
- Keep `CHANGELOG.md` and `VERSION` up to date
- Enforce `CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md`
